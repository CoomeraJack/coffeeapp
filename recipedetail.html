Weekly Coffee Inspiration

Viennese Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup hot espresso or strongly brewed coffee
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Sugar to taste optional
  • Chocolate shaving or cocoa powder for garnish, optional

Instructions

Step 1

Pour the hot coffee in a serving glass. Add the water and if desired, stir in a little sugar until dissolved.

Step 2

In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a whisk or a bowl with a hand mixer, beat the whipped cream until stiff peaks form.

Step 3

Gently pour or pipe the whipped cream over the back of a spoon held just above the coffee to form a layer on top. If desired, sprinkle with chocolate shavings or cocoa powder. Serve immediately.
